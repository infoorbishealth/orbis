Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

3 months ago

Orbis Health is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app and website.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Name and email (when you contact support or subscribe)

  • Habit details you choose to add

  • Routine names, notes, and reminders

  • Preferences and settings within the app

1.2 Automatically collected data

We collect basic usage data to improve performance:

  • Device type

  • App version

  • Interactions such as habit check-ins

  • Error logs and crash reports

We do not collect sensitive personal information.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Run core features (habit tracking, reminders, AI suggestions)

  • Personalize your routines and timing

  • Improve app performance and reliability

  • Provide customer support

  • Send important updates about the app

We never sell your data.

3. How your data Is stored

  • All data is stored securely on encrypted servers.

  • Only essential team members may access anonymized analytics.

  • We follow industry-standard protection measures.

4. Sharing of information

We only share data in these limited cases:

With service providers

For cloud hosting, analytics, or crash reporting — all under strict privacy agreements.

For legal compliance

If required by law, we may share necessary information.

We do not share your personal or habit data with advertisers.

5. Your rights

You can:

  • Access your data

  • Request deletion

  • Update or correct information

  • Opt out of analytics

  • Request a copy of your stored data

Email us at support@habitline.app for any of the above.

6. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality.
If you delete your account or send a request, your data will be permanently removed.

7. Children's privacy

Habitline is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

9. Contact us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us
